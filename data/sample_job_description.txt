Entry-Level Business Analyst

We are looking for an entry-level Business Analyst to support data reporting, market research, and business process improvement. The ideal candidate should have strong Excel skills, basic SQL knowledge, communication skills, and experience working on team projects.

Responsibilities include:
- Preparing reports
- Analyzing business data
- Supporting cross-functional teams
- Presenting findings to managers
- Maintaining Excel-based dashboards

Required qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in business, finance, information systems, or related field
- Excel skills including pivot tables and formulas
- Basic SQL knowledge
- Strong written and verbal communication
- Ability to work in a team environment
- Internship or project experience preferred
